Adele

Photo of Adele by Kristopher Harris on Flickr.

Adele is an English singer and songwriter; she has released three albums ever since her fame reached the states. Adele’s music consist of soul/folk tones, her voice is just out of this world.She has such a unique and smooth voice and yet so powerful at the same time.Adele in my opinion is the best recording music artist in today’s music culture.I love the fact that she stays within the soul genre but yet each album is completely different in conveying a story and sounds and her voice adds so much emotion to every song. Adele is definitely one of the best female recording artists out today.

By: Kathleen Rodriguez
